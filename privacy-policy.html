Introduction

Tobias Fu ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our Write-It-Down application ("App") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

Information We Collect

  • Notes and Categories: We store the notes and categories you create in the app. These are primarily stored locally on your device.

  • User Account Information: If you choose to create an account to use our public sharing features, we collect your email address and username.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With your consent

Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure.
