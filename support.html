Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a note?

Tap the "+" button at the bottom of the home screen, then select a category to create a new note.

How do I format text in my notes?

When editing a note, select the text you want to format. A formatting toolbar will appear with options for bold, italic, underline, and other text styles.

Can I add location to my notes?

Yes! When editing a note, tap the location button to add your current location or search for a specific place.

How do I organize my notes?

Write-It-Down uses categories to organize notes. You can create and customize categories from the Settings menu, and then assign notes to specific categories.

Are my notes private?

By default, all notes are private and stored only on your device. You can optionally choose to make specific notes public in the feed section.

Can I back up my notes?

Your notes are stored locally on your device and (if you choose to share them) in your account. We recommend regularly backing up your device to protect your data.
